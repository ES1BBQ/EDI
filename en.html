Log Entry

All fields are required! Current UTC time will be entered into log when Time field is left empty or when Time field is selected and Tab or Enter is pressed. Default value of 59 will be entered into selected RST fields when Tab or Enter is pressed.

Created Log rows display green and red buttons to the right of the row. Green button is for editing, Red button is for deleting log row.

Callsigns and Locator codes do not need to be entered in capital letters, this is done automatically.
